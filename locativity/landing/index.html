about

The future of productivity. Easily find out when and where you are most productive.

What you need

Use our iOS Client app that monitors your data and plugin into the productivity monitor api and a NinjaCourses account.

Results

At the end of each day, you'll be given an analysis of the days activities including a replay of your path and your time spent in each building. You can see how many lectures you've attended and where you were most productive.

Save Time

Over time, we will build models of your behavior and you'll start to see trends and ways to improve your efficiency. We could warn about the long lines at Qualcomm Cafe for example.
